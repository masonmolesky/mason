MUSICAL CAREER

“Music is the universal language of mankind” -Henry Wadsworth Longfellow (1807-1882).

Mason is an accomplished singer having performed across the U.S. and internationally. In fall 2022, Mason joined the Contate Concert Choir as a baritone. He previously was a baritone for the National Philharmonic Choir (pictured left) in Washington, DC for about 5 years as well as sang in the Alma College Choir where he toured in the U.S. Midwest, the U.K., and Ireland. Mason also sings at other one-off performances as well such as the 30th Anniversary of the Fall of the Berlin Wall. Mason is a member of Phi Mu Alpha (National Music Fraternity) and has also played violin/viola, trumpet, and percussion. As the pandemic comes to some semebence of an end, he hopes that chorale music can be used to bring the world together again.
