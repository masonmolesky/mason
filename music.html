MUSICAL CAREER

“Music is the universal language of mankind” -Henry Wadsworth Longfellow (1807-1882).

Mason is an accomplished singer having performed across the US and internationally. For almost 5 years until the pandemic, Mason was a Baritone for the National Philharmonic Choir (pictured left) in Washington, DC. Mason also sang at other one-off performances as well such as the 30th Anniversary of the Fall of the Berlin Wall. Previously he sang for the Alma College choir where he toured in the Midwest, Ireland, and the UK. Mason is a member of Phi Mu Alpha (National Music Fraternity) and has also played violin/viola, trumpet, and percussion. He looks forward to when the pandemic ends and chorale music can be used to bring the world together again.
