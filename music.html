MUSICAL CAREER

“Music is the universal language of mankind” -Henry Wadsworth Longfellow (1807-1882).

Mason is an accomplished singer having performed across the U.S. and internationally. In fall 2022, Mason joined the Contate Concert Choir as a baritone. He has also sung with the National Philharmonic Choir (pictured left) in Washington, DC since the fall of 2016 with a short break during the pandemic. Previously Mason sang in the Alma College Choir where he toured in the U.S. Midwest, the U.K., and Ireland. Mason also sings at other one-off performances as well such as the 30th Anniversary of the Fall of the Berlin Wall. Mason is a member of Phi Mu Alpha (National Music Fraternity) and has also played violin/viola, trumpet, and percussion. As we come to a new normal post pandemic, Mason hopes that music can be used to bring the world together.
