SOCCER COACHING

“Play hard, play smart, play together" -Pep Guardiola.

Soccer has been a life-long passion of mine, combining physical activity, teamwork, and strategy. While I no longer play competitively, I've found deep fulfillment in coaching - espeically within the DC Public School System. With 10 seasons of experience coaching at the elementary, middle school, and high school levels, I’ve enjoyed the unique challenges and rewards that come with each stage of player development.

If you have a child in D.C. interested in further developing their soccer skills, check out your school's soccer program and/or Washington Capital United.

MUSICAL CAREER

“Music is the universal language of mankind” -Henry Wadsworth Longfellow (1807-1882).

I love the ability for music to bring people together and I have been very fortunate to sing with incredible people in various choirs with performances across the U.S. and internationally. As a baritone, I have sung for the Contate Concert Choir 2022-2023, the National Philharmonic Choir (pictured left) 2016-2023, and the Alma College Choir. I also sing at other one-off performances such as the 30th Anniversary of the Fall of the Berlin Wall. I am also a member of Phi Mu Alpha (National Music Fraternity) and previously played violin/viola, trumpet, and percussion.
